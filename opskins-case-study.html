'Lette Macarons

Redesign E-Commerce Checkout

VIEW PROTOTYPE


The Brief

Website Redesign to improve e-commerce checkout process.
This project is not affiliated with 'Lette Macarons. The following work is a conceptual exercise to educate and refine UX/UI skills.

‘Lette Macarons is a franchise of shops selling gourmet macarons throughout California and Texas. Their boutique approach captures the trend of “Instagramable foods” through their colorful myriad of unique flavors and personalized packaging of gift boxes and ribbons. ‘Lette Macarons believes in unique and interesting experiences which can be seen in the unconventional design of their website experience.


Challenge

The client, 'Lette Macarons would like a redesign of their e-commerce website's navigation.

Solution

A clickable prototype that eases information density, guides users via clear hierarchy of categories, and create quick and efficient checkout experience.

My Roles

C&C analysis, heuristic analysis, personas, user flow, card sorting, paper prototype, high-fidelity InvisionApp prototype.

Conditions
  • 2 weeks at General Assembly.
Tools
  • SketchApp, InvisionApp, Krita, Material Palette, Google Drive, whiteboard, markers, Post-It notes
