What is a Nicole?

I was a certified paralegal in a previous life, but something was missing; what was my value to the world? Even Clark Kent has to leave his cubicle to make a difference, so I reconnected with my root interest in helping people, being creative, and advocating for those who cannot and found myself in UX. It's been a wild ride, but this is where I've found my calling to make the world a better place.

When I'm not working or volunteering, I enjoy playing League of Legends (Cardboard-Tier Irelia main, ya'll), reviewing games for the online zine, ConFreaks & Geeks, and practicing cardistry.
